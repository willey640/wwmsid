Book Summary

Have you ever wondered what goes on behind the scenes of a Dive Shop or what it takes to become and work as a Scuba Diving Professional? This book is great for those who enjoy dive stories, jokes, and practical advice. Even if you have never dove before, you will find something in this audiobook that will spark your interest in diving. I share my many experiences from first trying Scuba to finding a dive job in a tropical paradise. I have learned so much since my first breath underwater and hope to share with you all of the tips that will make you become the best diver you can be!
