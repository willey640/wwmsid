Welcome!

My name is Christopher Willey. I had a dynamic career in diving but unfortunately, I am no longer able to dive due to a medical condition. I wrote "What Would My Scuba Instructor Do?" — available on Amazon and Audible. The book shares stories, jokes, and real-world diving advice.

Career Journey

I trained and certified over 250 divers across multiple shops in Roatan, Honduras. I discovered the “Island Fueler” shipwreck and created PADI specialties like Lionfish Hunter and Advanced Fish ID.
