Welcome!

My name is Christopher Willey. I am a multi-talented professional with a dynamic career spanning various industries, including diving, security, retail, geotechnical work, content creation, mining, writing, and more.

Career Journey

At the end of my diving instructor career, I trained and certified over 250 divers. I have worked as a Divemaster, Instructor Trainer, and Dive Center Manager across multiple dive shops in Roatan, Honduras.

My retail management experience at Canadian Tire, Boart Longyear, and Westburne Electric strengthened my customer service, sales, and leadership skills.

I’ve also worked in geotechnical roles for mining companies, focusing on core sampling and assisting geologists with operational needs.

Certifications and Skills

  • Diving Certifications: PADI Master Instructor, Tec Deep Instructor, Freediving Instructor, and more.
  • Professional Licenses: Alberta Class 4 Driver’s License, Manitoba Prospecting License, Drone Pilot Certification.
  • Security and Safety: First Aid & CPR, Alberta Security Training, WHMIS, Fall Protection, Fire Safety, Mental Health Awareness.
  • Technical Skills: Python, smart contract development (Cardano), HTML/CSS, social media management, scuba equipment maintenance.

Personal Projects and Interests

I authored the book What Would My Scuba Instructor Do? and enjoy hands-on projects like creating custom wire reels for drills.

Core Values

My approach is rooted in continuous learning, adaptability, and helping others—whether mentoring new divers or leading teams.
