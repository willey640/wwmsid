Welcome!

My name is Christopher Willey. I had a dynamic career in diving but unfortunately, I am no longer able to dive due to a medical condition. I have written a book titled "What Would My Scuba Instructor Do?" which is available on Amazon and Audible. The book is a collection of stories, jokes, and practical advice for divers and those interested in the diving industry.

Career Journey

At the end of my diving instructor career, I trained and certified over 250 divers. I have worked as a Divemaster, Instructor Trainer, and Dive Center Manager across multiple dive shops in Roatan, Honduras. I found a missing shipwreck "Island Fueler", trained and certified over 250 divers, and created a PADI Distinctive Specialty Course called Lionfish Hunter. I also created a PADI Advanced Fish ID Specialty Course.
