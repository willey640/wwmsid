Welcome to WWMSID

WWMSID is built around scuba stories, professional diving experience, dive-shop lessons, and the book What Would My Scuba Instructor Do?

About the Author

Christopher Willey

My name is Christopher Willey. I had a dynamic career in diving, but unfortunately, I am no longer able to dive due to a medical condition.

I wrote What Would My Scuba Instructor Do?, available on Amazon and Audible. The book shares stories, jokes, real-world diving advice, and a look inside the scuba industry.

The purpose of WWMSID is to share the humour, lessons, challenges, and unusual moments that come from working around dive shops, students, instructors, travel, and the underwater world.

Dive Career

Career Journey

My diving career included scuba instruction, specialty training, dive-shop work, underwater education, and media attention connected to lionfish and marine awareness.

Scuba Instruction

Trained and certified more than 250 divers across multiple dive shops in Roatan, Honduras.

Shipwreck Discovery

Discovered the “Island Fueler” shipwreck and contributed to local diving history and exploration.

PADI Specialties

Created specialty training connected to Lionfish Hunter and Advanced Fish ID education.
